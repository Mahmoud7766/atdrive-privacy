Privacy Policy – Rider App (AT DRIVE)

Effective Date: May 2026 | Last Updated: July 2026

At AT DRIVE, we are committed to our responsibility to protect our riders' privacy, and we pledge to process and safeguard their personal information in accordance with the highest data security standards and Google Play Store developer policies.

1. Information We Collect and Process

We collect the following data to ensure the proper operation of our ride-hailing services and to provide a seamless and secure ride experience:

  • Personal Account Data: Full name, mobile phone number, and email address.
  • Location Data: Live and precise tracking of the user's geographical position to determine pickup and drop-off points.
  • Transaction and Trip Data: History of previous trips, routing details, and estimated or actual fare amounts.
  • Payment Information: Trip payments, wallet balance, completed transactions, refunds, and payment records where applicable.
  • App and Device Data: Device model, operating system version, application version, Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token, and technical information required to provide notifications, improve security, and maintain application performance.
  • Diagnostics Data: Crash reports and performance information used only to improve app stability and fix technical issues.

2. Foreground Location Disclosure

Important Notice for Google Play Reviewers and Users:
The AT DRIVE Rider application collects precise location data only while the rider is actively using the application and while the application is running in the foreground.

The application uses only foreground location permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) and does not request background location access (ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION).

The application immediately stops collecting location data when the rider closes the application or when the trip has ended. The application does not collect location data in the background under any circumstances.

3. How We Use the Information

The data collected is utilized strictly for the following purposes:

  • Providing dispatching and ride-booking services, making it easy for the rider to meet the assigned driver.
  • Tracking the ride path in real-time during transit to ensure the rider's personal safety, security, and risk prevention.
  • Offering rapid technical support and resolving customer complaints through our support team.
  • Enhancing overall application infrastructure and optimizing future user experiences.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

  • We do not sell or lease riders' personal data to any third party for advertising, marketing, or promotional purposes.
  • The rider's name, profile photo (if available), phone number, pickup location, destination, and live location during an active trip are shared only with the assigned driver for trip operation, navigation, communication, and rider safety.
  • Authorized AT DRIVE personnel may access limited account and support information only when necessary for customer support, fraud prevention, legal compliance, account verification, or dispute resolution.
  • The application integrates with secure third-party service providers, including:
    • Firebase Authentication: To securely manage rider account authentication and login.
    • Cloud Firestore and Firebase Realtime Database: To securely store and synchronize account information, trip requests, ride status, wallet data (where applicable), and real-time trip information.
    • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): To deliver ride updates, driver arrival notifications, security alerts, and service announcements.
    • Firebase Storage: To securely store rider profile images and other uploaded files where applicable.
    • Google Maps Platform: To display maps, calculate routes, estimate trip distance and duration, determine pickup and destination locations, and provide navigation-related functionality.
    • Firebase Crashlytics (Google), when enabled to collect crash reports and diagnostic information for improving application stability: Used solely to detect application crashes, improve stability, monitor performance, and troubleshoot technical issues.

5. Data Protection and Security Measures

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal data, including secure cloud infrastructure, encrypted HTTPS/TLS communication, restricted access controls, and industry-standard security measures.

Although no system can guarantee absolute security, we continuously work to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. User Rights and Data Deletion Requirements

Riders retain complete control over their personal data in accordance with Google Play Store guidelines:

  • Users may request permanent deletion of their account and personal data at any time through the Settings page inside the application or by visiting the Rider Account Deletion webpage.
  • Permanent Account Deletion Request: The app provides a direct and explicit option inside the Settings menu enabling users to "Delete Account and Data". Upon submitting this request, or upon visiting our external deletion landing page: Rider Account Deletion Request Link, we permanently erase all personal details, trip history, and logged locations from our active servers within a period not exceeding 30 days.

7. Data Retention Period

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services. After account deletion, personal information is deleted or anonymized from our active systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable law, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, financial compliance, security, or regulatory obligations.
