Privacy Policy – Rider App (AT DRIVE)

Effective Date: May 2026 | Last Updated: June 2026

At AT DRIVE, we are committed to our responsibility to protect our riders' privacy, and we pledge to process and safeguard their personal information in accordance with the highest data security standards and Google Play Store developer policies.

1. Information We Collect and Process

We collect the following data to ensure the proper operation of our ride-hailing services and to provide a seamless and secure ride experience:

  • Personal Account Data: Full name, mobile phone number, and email address.
  • Location Data: Live and precise tracking of the user's geographical position to determine pickup and drop-off points.
  • Transaction and Trip Data: History of previous trips, routing details, and estimated or actual fare amounts.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique hardware identifiers to optimize application performance and compatibility.

2. Location Data Mechanisms

The Rider application inherently relies on location tracking to offer its primary functionalities. We clarify our collection mechanism as follows:

Precise location data is collected **only while the application is active and running in the foreground (Foreground Location)**. This is necessary to pinpoint the rider’s exact departure spot, streamline pairing with the closest available captain, and compute path routing along with estimated trip fares. **We never track or collect the rider’s location after the app is closed or once the active trip has safely concluded.**

3. How We Use the Information

The data collected is utilized strictly for the following purposes:

  • Providing dispatching and ride-booking services, making it easy for the rider to meet the assigned driver.
  • Tracking the ride path in real-time during transit to ensure the rider's personal safety, security, and risk prevention.
  • Offering rapid technical support and resolving customer complaints through our support team.
  • Enhancing overall application infrastructure and optimizing future user experiences.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

  • We do not sell or lease our riders' personal data to any external entities for promotional, marketing, or advertising use.
  • The rider's name, profile photo (if available), and current location are shared solely with the assigned driver during an active trip to allow them to locate the rider and complete the ride.
  • The application integrates with cloud-based third-party services that fully comply with modern security standards:
    • Firebase (Google): For managing and securing user authentication, real-time databases, and live push notifications.
    • Google Maps Platform: For rendering map interfaces, tracking routes, and processing geographic calculations.

5. Data Protection and Security Measures

We enforce strict technical and organizational safety measures, including encrypted communication protocols (HTTPS) and cloud servers shielded by advanced firewalls, to protect your data from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure, or destruction.

6. User Rights and Data Deletion Requirements

Riders retain complete control over their personal data in accordance with Google Play Store guidelines:

  • Users have the right to modify their personal info at any time from within the account edit screen in the app.
  • Permanent Account Deletion Request: The app provides a direct and explicit option inside the Settings menu enabling users to "Delete Account and Data". Upon submitting this request, or upon visiting our external deletion landing page: Rider Account Deletion Request Link, we permanently erase all personal details, trip history, and logged locations from our active servers within a period not exceeding 30 days.

7. Amendments to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically to align with legal amendments, technological shifts, or operational changes required by application stores. Any revised version will be published instantly on this page.
