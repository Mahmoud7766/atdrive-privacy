Privacy Policy - Driver/Captain Application (AT DRIVE)

Effective Date: May 2026 | Last Updated: June 2026

At AT DRIVE, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our partner drivers (captains). This policy explains how we collect, use, share, retain, and delete data to operate the service safely and in accordance with applicable Google Play user data requirements.

1. Information and Documents We Collect

To verify accounts, operate trips, provide support, and ensure platform safety, we may collect the following data:

  • Basic Account Data: Full name, phone number, email address, profile picture, and account login information.
  • Official Verification Documents: Driver's license, vehicle registration, criminal record check, and other documents required to verify identity and driver eligibility.
  • Vehicle Data: Vehicle type, model, plate number, vehicle photos, and related vehicle profile details.
  • Geographical Location Data: Precise device location, real-time updates, trip routes, and location history collected only while the captain is Online and the app is running in the foreground or as a foreground service, and during active trips.
  • Financial Transaction Data: Completed ride history, earnings, balances, wallet records, pending payments, and financial dispute records.
  • App and Device Data: Push notification tokens such as Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens, device identifiers or app instance identifiers where applicable, app status, and technical data needed to send notifications and maintain account security.
  • Ratings, Support, and Safety Data: Ratings, reviews, complaints, support messages, safety reports, fraud-prevention records, and account activity logs.
  • Diagnostics Data: Crash logs, performance data, and diagnostic information if such services are enabled, used to improve app reliability and troubleshoot technical issues.

2. Foreground Location Disclosure

Important Notice for Google Play Reviewers and Users:
The AT DRIVE Captain application collects precise location data while the captain is actively using the application and while the application is running in the foreground or as a foreground service after the captain sets their status to "Online". The application uses only foreground location permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) and does not request ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION. The application does not collect location data when the foreground service is stopped or the user explicitly logs out/goes offline or when the foreground service is stopped.

Location information is collected only to operate the driver service and only while the captain is available for work or during an active trip:

  • To allow the captain to receive nearby ride requests while the application is running in the foreground or as an active foreground service.
  • To display the captain's live location during an accepted trip.
  • To calculate trip distance and duration accurately.
  • To improve passenger safety and support trip dispute resolution.

The application stops collecting location data when the captain goes offline or when the application is completely closed by the user.

We never sell or share location data with advertising or marketing companies. All transmitted location data is protected using HTTPS/TLS encryption and secure cloud infrastructure.

3. How We Use Your Information and Documents

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Verifying the driver's legal and professional eligibility before activating their account on the platform.
  • Managing driver availability, matching nearby ride requests while the captain is Online, and operating real-time trips.
  • Collecting location data only while the application is active or running as a foreground service to support ride matching and trip management.
  • Showing the matched passenger the captain's name, profile photo, vehicle details, phone number, and live location during an active trip.
  • Sending push notifications about ride requests, trip status, account updates, safety notices, and support messages.
  • Calculating financial entitlements, earnings, balances, wallet records, and trip-related payments.
  • Providing customer support, handling complaints, resolving disputes, and improving service quality.
  • Enhancing safety, preventing fraud, enforcing platform rules, and investigating misuse or unauthorized activity.
  • Improving app stability and performance through diagnostics, crash reports, and technical troubleshooting where applicable.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • We do not sell or rent captains' personal documents or personal data to third-party marketing or advertising companies.
  • The captain's name, profile photo, phone number, vehicle details, and real-time location are shared only with the matched passenger during an active trip to facilitate pickup, trip operation, communication, and safety.
  • Authorized AT DRIVE personnel may access limited account, document, support, and safety data only when needed for account review, activation, support, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.
  • The application uses third-party service providers to operate core app features, including:
    • Firebase Authentication: To manage secure account authentication.
    • Cloud Firestore and Firebase Realtime Database: To store and synchronize account, ride, wallet, availability, and real-time trip data.
    • Firebase Storage: To store uploaded profile, vehicle, and verification document images.
    • Firebase Cloud Messaging: To send ride requests, account alerts, and service notifications.
    • Google Maps Platform: To provide maps, route guidance, live location display, and navigation-related features.
    • Diagnostics and crash reporting services (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics) are used solely to collect crash reports and performance metrics to improve app stability. To detect crashes, improve stability, and resolve technical issues.

5. Data Security and Protection

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal data, including access controls, secure cloud infrastructure, and encryption in transit using HTTPS/TLS where applicable. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to limit access to authorized personnel and service providers who need the data for the purposes described in this policy.

Location data is transmitted using encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections, and access is restricted to authorized systems and personnel only.

6. Driver Rights, Account and Data Deletion

AT DRIVE is committed to enabling captains to control their personal data in accordance with applicable Google Play user data requirements:

  • Captains can request the permanent deletion of their account and personal data at any time by navigating to the Settings menu inside the app and clicking "Delete Account", or by visiting the dedicated web deletion request link: Captain Account Deletion Link.
  • What happens upon a deletion request? Once confirmed, we delete personal data, official documents, and trip-related location records from active systems within a period not exceeding 30 days, except for data that we must retain for legal, regulatory, security, fraud-prevention, financial compliance, pending transaction, or dispute-resolution purposes.

7. Data Retention Period

We retain personal data and documents for as long as the captain's account is active or as needed to provide the service. After account deletion or prolonged inactivity, we delete or anonymize data from active systems within the stated deletion period, except for records retained for as long as required by applicable law or as necessary to resolve disputes, prevent fraud, enforce agreements, maintain financial records, or comply with regulatory obligations.
