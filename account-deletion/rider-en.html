Rider Account Deletion

Rider Account Deletion Request
The AT DRIVE application allows riders to permanently delete their account and associated personal data in accordance with strict Google Play Store requirements and our approved privacy and user protection policy.

When Will Your Data Be Deleted?

Account deletion requests are processed, and data is completely wiped from our servers within a timeframe not exceeding 30 days from the date the request is submitted. In most standard scenarios, the deletion occurs much faster.

How to Delete Your Account from Within the App

  1. Open the AT DRIVE Rider application.
  2. Go to the Settings menu (Profile page).
  3. Select the option "Delete Account and Data".
  4. Confirm your final permanent deletion request.
  5. The system will automatically schedule your account for comprehensive removal.

Data That Will Be Permanently Deleted

  • Full name, mobile number, and email address.
  • Profile picture and essential account configuration data.
  • Saved addresses and favorite historical locations.
  • Detailed trip logs and geographic routes associated with the account.
  • Any other identifying parameters or metadata stored during app usage.

Data We May Be Obligated to Retain Temporarily

Under regulatory and legal policies, certain limited financial records or invoices may be retained for a specified period if necessary to comply with local corporate laws, prevent fraud, or settle outstanding financial disputes related to credit cards and the application wallet.
