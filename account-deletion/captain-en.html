Captain Account Deletion

Driver Account Deletion Request

The AT DRIVE platform provides captains with the ability to permanently delete their account and associated personal data in accordance with Google Play requirements and data protection policies outlining store user rights.

Important Notice:
Once the deletion process is executed, the captain will not be able to recover the account or any associated data. It will require creating an entirely new account if you wish to use the platform again.

How to Delete Your Account via the App

  1. Open the AT DRIVE Captain app.
  2. Go to the Settings page.
  3. Select "Delete Account".
  4. Review the final confirmation message.
  5. Tap "Confirm Account Deletion".
The deletion request is sent directly to the system, and the permanent removal of data associated with the account from active servers will be scheduled immediately.

Request Account Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the application, you can send an account deletion request from the email address registered with your account to:

mahmoud12958@gmail.com

Please include: Captain Account Deletion Request in the subject line of the email.

Data That Will Be Permanently Deleted

  • Full name, phone number, and email address.
  • Profile picture and basic profile details.
  • Vehicle information, driver's license, and vehicle registration documents.
  • Official documents previously uploaded for identity verification purposes.
  • Geographical location history recorded and linked to the account.
  • Detailed activity logs within the app.

Data We May Retain Temporarily

Certain financial or legal records may be retained for a limited period if required by local laws or for the following specific purposes:

  • Resolving financial disputes or pending transactions in the wallet.
  • Preventing fraud and combating abuse across the network.
  • Complying with local legal and regulatory obligations.

Deletion Processing Timeframe

Account deletion requests are executed, and all active servers are completely sanitized within a period not exceeding 30 days from the date the request is submitted or confirmed.
