Captain Account Deletion

Driver & Captain Account Deletion Request Portal

The AT DRIVE platform provides captains with a clear and direct mechanism to permanently delete their account and associated personal data. This portal is maintained in absolute compliance with Google Play Store User Data Safety requirements, respecting data protection rules and giving our team of captains full rights to data minimization.

Important Notice:
Once the account deletion process is executed, it is definitive and absolute. The captain will not be able to recover the profile history, tier metrics, wallet archives, or any associated documentation. If you decide to rejoin the platform in the future, you will have to undergo an entirely new registration and background check process.

How to Delete Your Account via the Application

The fastest and most automated way to terminate your account is directly from within your mobile application interface:

  1. Open the AT DRIVE Captain application on your mobile device.
  2. Navigate to the Settings or Profile Management menu.
  3. Locate and click on the "Delete Account" or "Account Safety" option.
  4. Carefully read the final cautionary verification prompt.
  5. Confirm your execution by clicking "Confirm Account Deletion".
Your deletion request will be safely pushed to our central servers immediately. The account status will switch to inactive, and total erasure from active data servers will be scheduled right away.

Data Subject to Permanent Erasure

Upon verifying and approving the removal request, the following records will be totally wiped out from our operational production environments:

  • Full legal name, authenticated mobile phone number, and verified email address.
  • Profile photos, identity verification images, and avatars.
  • Vehicle specifications, driver's license copies, vehicle registration records, and insurance documentation.
  • Historical background checks and commercial files previously submitted for verification.
  • Historical route telemetry, past pickup logs, and granular GPS location logs associated with the profile.

Data We May Retain Dynamically / Temporarily

Please note that AT DRIVE will not keep your data for commercial marketing. However, certain records cannot be dropped immediately due to statutory financial or security laws. Such specific subsets include:

  • Financial transactions, wallet ledgers, or outstanding balances to settle pending payout reporting.
  • Strict audit tokens necessary to combat active fraud, security abuses, or platform bans.
  • Compliance archives required by local laws, public transportation regulations, or legitimate legal court orders.

Processing Timeframes

In full adherence to Google Play data mandates, manual and automated deletion queues are processed, fully sanitized, and cleared across all operational databases within a strict timeframe not exceeding 30 days from the initial request confirmation date.
